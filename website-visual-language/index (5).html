San Luis Chamber Orchestra

Who We Are

We are a community orchestra of volunteer musicians, together for the love of the art

Started with the mission to provide opportunities to those who want to play music more accessibly. We believe great orchestral music belongs to everyone — performer and audience alike — right here in San Luis Obispo.

For Musicians

Join Us

Play an instrument? Looking for Community? Join Us!

We meet every Wednesday night from 6:00–8:00pm. We are open to all types of musicians. No audition required — just a love of music and a willingness to show up and play.

  • Adult
  • Youth
  • College Students
  • We are a great non-competitive alternative to Cal Poly Orchestra

Support the Arts

Help Keep Music Alive in SLO

Your generosity makes it possible for the San Luis Chamber Orchestra to bring live music to our community. Every contribution directly supports our volunteer musicians and programs.

San Luis Chamber Orchestra is a nonprofit. All donations are tax-deductible. You will receive an email receipt.
